How to add or edit mentors

All mentor data lives in your Google Sheet. The portal reads it automatically — no code needed.

  1. Open your Google Sheet ↗
  2. Add or edit a row — one mentor per row
  3. Come back here and click Refresh from sheet

How to add photos

Upload the photo to the shared Drive folder ↗, then paste a direct image URL into the photo column.

To get the direct URL from a Drive file:

  1. Right-click the photo → Get link → copy it
  2. Your link looks like:
    https://drive.google.com/file/d/FILE_ID/view
  3. Change it to:
    https://drive.google.com/thumbnail?id=FILE_ID&sz=w400
  4. Paste that into the photo column
